## Suggested 2–3 minute script
1. **Case queue** — open the app, point out the 34 flagged cases sorted by priority
   (Critical → Low) and the persistent "pending review" status.
2. **Run an investigation** — select a Critical case (e.g. a sanctioned-jurisdiction
   transfer) and click **Run investigation**. Narrate the live reasoning trace as each
   of the six agents streams in.
3. **Evidence & citations** — open the *Evidence & Citations* tab; show the KYC profile,
   the computed behavioural facts, and the exact cited transactions.
4. **Typology & regulation** — show the ranked typology match, confidence meter, drivers,
   and the RAG-retrieved regulatory context.
5. **Narrative & verification** — show the drafted EDD narrative and the Verifier's
   per-claim checks (all tracing back to evidence).
6. **Guardrail demo** — mention the Verifier catches hallucinated figures/citations and
   triggers a deterministic retry (covered by `test_verifier_flags_unsupported_claim`).
7. **Human approval gate** — approve / edit / reject; show the decision persisting and
   appearing in the *Audit Log* tab.
8. **Theme** — toggle light/dark to show the polished, accessible UI.
